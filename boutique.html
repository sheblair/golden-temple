Boutique

Our boutique is stocked with rare and unique gifts from across the globe. We carry everything from candles, incense, and specialty herbs to books, journals, and a vast selection of tarot and oracle decks. We also carry musical instruments, statues from around the world, and we have an entire section just for children! Many of our clothes and accessories are imported from Nepal, Thailand, India, and Africa.

Our consignment section, stocked by boutique buyer Lisa Hickman and local arist Karen Graffeo, carries a curated selection of vintage and gently used items from such far flung places as New York and Italy. Finally, our crystal case contains the largest selection of crystals and gemstones in the state of Alabama, and our sterling silver jewelry collection is second to none.

Whether you are shopping for yourself or a loved one, we know you'll be enchanted by the wide variety of intriguing and beautiful items you'll find in our boutique.
