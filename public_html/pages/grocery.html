Natural Grocery

Golden Temple Natural Grocery is Birmingham's only source for a completely vegetarian grocery experience.

We carry a wide array of foods including canned goods, frozen foods, raw food products and vegan snacks. Looking for a hard-to-find item? We can special order numerous food items for all your health needs. Gluten-free, dairy-free and vegan items are our specialty.

In addition to our grocery, we also carry tremendous selection of natural hair and skin care products.
