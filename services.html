Join Kat on Mondays for 30 minutes of guided meditation + breathwork in the yoga space. Generally translated as breath control, pranayama consists of techniques designed to gain mastery over the respiratory process while recognizing the connection between the breath, the mind, and the emotions. Ultimately, pranayama serves as a potent bridge between the outward, active state of doing and the internal, surrendering practice of meditation and observation. We will explore various modalities and techniques each week, Leave feeling calm and centered - no experience necessary!

Massage

Golden Temple offers massage therapy from licensed massage therapist Michele Nailen. Known as Crossroads Healing Massage, Michele’s massage therapy offers treatment for a multitude of issues. According to studies from the University of Miami School of Medicine Touch Therapy Institute, massage provides numerous health benefits. Massage can lead to reductions in stress and alleviation of pain. It can also improve immune function, lower blood pressure and reduce migraine headaches.


 

Michele became a licensed massage therapist in 2004, attending Virginia College in Birmingham and graduating with top honors. She has an Occupational Associate Degree in Therapeutic Massage. She has extensive experience in the field of healing, and has worked in the health food industry for over 30 years. She is also a Level 1 Reiki practitioner.

She has extensive experience working with clients in the LGBTQIA+ community, and has signed the HAES (Health At Every Size) pledge and is listed on their registry. She is also currently working on becoming a certified intuitive eating counselor.

Call Michele today at (205) 329-0180 or visit her website at www.amtamembers.com/michelenailen to learn more or schedule an appointment

Tarot

Tarot is an ancient form of divination practiced for centuries throughout Europe and later in the Americas. Employing a specialized deck of cards, the reader guides an individual through the mysterious labyrinth of the self, allowing the subject to better understand their path, gaining clarity – from the shadowy recesses of the past, through the often confusing present, to aiding the subject in understanding what might yet come. While mainly used as a form of entertainment today, one can better understand ones goals, motives and aspirations by use of the Tarot and one skilled at interpreting the complex symbols contained in the cards.
