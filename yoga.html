Join Kat on Mondays for 30 minutes of guided meditation + breathwork in the yoga space. Generally translated as breath control, pranayama consists of techniques designed to gain mastery over the respiratory process while recognizing the connection between the breath, the mind, and the emotions. Ultimately, pranayama serves as a potent bridge between the outward, active state of doing and the internal, surrendering practice of meditation and observation. We will explore various modalities and techniques each week, Leave feeling calm and centered - no experience necessary!

Bridgette René has been teaching Hatha style yoga since 2014. A long time health and fitness advocate, she earned 200 hour yoga teaching certifications from Peace Through Yoga, in Indianapolis, IN and Akshi Yogashala in Rishikesh, India. Bridgette also teaches Kemetic Yoga and is a certified Thai Yoga Bodywork practitioner. Bridgette’s yoga classes and Thai Yoga Bodywork aim to improve overall well-being, balance and flexibility while reducing stress and discomfort. She is known for her authentic, non-judgmental teaching style.
